Welcome

This website is designed for people who have been diagnosed with heart failure or have been advised that their heart valve condition may cause the body to hold extra fluid.

This tool aims to support you in recognising changes in your symptoms and managing your condition day-to-day.

It does not replace advice from your healthcare team. Always follow your personal care plan.

If you have severe breathlessness, chest pain, fainting, or collapse, please seek urgent medical attention or call 999.
